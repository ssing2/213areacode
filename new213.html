About 213 Area Code

Area code 213 serves Downtown Los Angeles and was one of the original area codes introduced in 1947. It covers the heart of LA including the central business district, entertainment areas, and historic neighborhoods like Koreatown and Westlake.

Key Facts:

  • Introduced: 1947 (Original California area code)
  • Type: Original area code, now overlaid with 323
  • Primary Use: Business, government, downtown services
  • Population Served: 4+ million LA Metro residents
LA Area Code Evolution
  • 213 (Original - 1947)
  • 818 (San Fernando - 1984)
  • 310 (West Side - 1991)
  • 323 (Overlay - 1998)
  • 747 (Latest - 2009)
  • 424 (West Side - 2006)
